Our Heritage

Founded in 2010, The Petit Four Original has established itself as a purveyor of exceptional pastries crafted with time-honored techniques and the finest ingredients available.

Our philosophy is rooted in the traditional French approach to pastry-making, blending classical methods with innovative flavors to create a unique culinary experience that transcends the ordinary.
