Our Heritage

Founded in 2010, The Petit Four Original has established itself as a purveyor of exceptional pastries crafted with time-honored techniques and the finest ingredients available.

Our philosophy is rooted in the traditional French approach to pastry-making, blending classical methods with innovative flavors to create a unique culinary experience that transcends the ordinary.

Our Story

Bakery History

A Journey of Passion

The Petit Four Original began as a small family operation with a simple mission: to bring authentic French pastry techniques to our community. What started as a modest bakery has grown into a celebrated establishment known for its unwavering commitment to quality and innovation.

Our journey over the past decade has been filled with challenges, triumphs, and countless hours of perfecting our craft. We've expanded our offerings while staying true to our founding principles of authenticity and excellence.

Our Milestones

  • 2010

    Founding of The Petit Four Original

    Opened our first small bakery with a focus on traditional French petit fours.

  • 2013

    First Recognition

    Awarded "Best New Bakery" by the Regional Culinary Association.

  • 2015

    Expansion

    Moved to our current larger location and expanded our menu offerings.

  • 2018

    International Recognition

    Chef Sophie won Gold Medal at the International Pastry Competition.

  • 2020

    10 Year Anniversary

    Celebrated a decade of excellence with the introduction of our anniversary collection.
