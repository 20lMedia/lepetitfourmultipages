Chef Sophie Laurent

Master Pâtissier

With over 15 years of experience in fine pastry arts, Chef Sophie brings her passion and expertise to every creation at The Petit Four Original.

After training at the prestigious École de Pâtisserie in Paris and working in renowned bakeries across Europe, Chef Sophie returned to her hometown to share her culinary vision with the community.

"I believe that pastry is where science meets art. It requires precision and technique, but also creativity and soul. Each petit four tells a story and brings joy to those who taste it."

The Culinary Journey

Training and Inspiration

Chef Sophie's journey began in Paris, where she studied under some of the most renowned pâtissiers in France. Her formal education at the École de Pâtisserie formed a solid foundation, but it was her apprenticeship with Master Chef Jean-Pierre Dubois that truly shaped her approach to pastry making.

"Chef Dubois taught me that exceptional pastry requires both technical perfection and an artistic soul," says Sophie. "He showed me how to respect traditional methods while embracing innovation."

Global Influences

After completing her training in Paris, Sophie embarked on a culinary tour, working in bakeries throughout France, Belgium, and Switzerland. Each location added new dimensions to her culinary perspective, from the precise chocolate work of Belgium to the innovative flavor combinations found in modern Swiss patisseries.
